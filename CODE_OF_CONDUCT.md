# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and inclusive community.

Expected behavior:
1. Be constructive and specific in technical discussions.
2. Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
3. Focus on issues, evidence, and reproducible facts.
4. Avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, threats, or hate speech.
2. Publishing private information without consent.
3. Repeated disruptive behavior in issues/PRs/discussions.
4. Any conduct that creates an unsafe environment.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take moderation actions including:
1. warning
2. comment removal
3. temporary restriction
4. permanent ban for severe/repeated violations

## Reporting

For conduct violations, use private maintainer contact channels instead of public escalation.
